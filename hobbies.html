MOVIES

My favorite thing about The Office is the mockumentary style of filming. It is so cool when the characters talk meta and comment on their own stories.

NATURE

I like wide open spaces, clouds, waterfalls and fields. When the world is that big, nothing could be so terribly bad.
I really like wildlife shows and documentaries on nature. I have always found biology an interesting subject. Talking about dragonflies, sitting in a meadow overlooking a lake would be such an ideal way to spend an afternoon with a friend.
